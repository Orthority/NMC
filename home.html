Introduction

Welcome to my journal documenting my experience during the precouncil and council exams for nursing.

In the pursuit of becoming a registered nurse, the journey of a nursing student is laden with challenges, victories, and an abundance of learning experiences.


This journal serves as a chronicle of my odyssey through the precouncil and council exams, encapsulating the trials, triumphs, and invaluable lessons learned along the way.

I vividly recall the uncertainty and apprehension that accompanied the preparation for these pivotal exams.


It is my sincere hope that this journal will serve as a beacon of guidance for student nurses, illuminating the path toward success and easing the burdens of preparation.

May this journal serve as a testament to the resilience, dedication, and unwavering spirit of student nurses at Obafemi Awolowo University.

Reflections

  1. Analyzing the collected data provided valuable insights into the research questions. I gained proficiency in statistical analysis techniques and the interpretation of results, enhancing my analytical skills.
  2. Communication is key, carry your supervisor along everything you are about to start and end.
  3. I learned the importance of clarity, coherence, and adhering to academic writing standards.
  4. Navigating the research process involved time management, patience and persistence. Understanding guidelines from supervisor is key, addressing comments, and revising the corrections accordingly were essential steps
  5. Don't believe everything the printer/binder says, always check up progress on-site to see with your eyes.
  6. Conducting inferential statistical tests such as chi-square tests and regression analysis provided valuable insights into the associations between variables in the study.
  7. Utilizing SPSS for data analysis facilitated the application of statistical techniques and the interpretation of results in the context of the study objectives.
  8. Submit finalized articles summarizing the research findings, including detailed analysis, interpretation, and follow corrections from supervisor.
